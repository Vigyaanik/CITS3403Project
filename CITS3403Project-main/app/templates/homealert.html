MEMORY


In an age where information is so easily accessable, we dont often have the need to remember much information as at any point we can just search on the internet for whatever we want.

What many people don't realise is that besides making you more knowledgeable, having a good memory has also shown to improve:
  • Social Skills
  • Ability to Learn
  • Humour
  • Attention
  • Creativity
The world record for most random objects memorized is held by Nischal Narayanam who set the record at just 10 years old. Whilst we can't all be the next Nischal Narayanam, we can learn ways to help us remember information better.

This site will teach you about some of the factors that effect memory and has some games to help demonstrate each of these.

After completing the content and the games, you will have a greater understanding of how memory works, how to remember information better and communicate memorable information. Head over to the content page to begin.
